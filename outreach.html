Scientific Outreach and Communication

Alongside research and teaching, I am committed to making science accessible, equitable, and engaging for audiences of all ages. I have developed and led outreach programs that reach K–12 classrooms, public audiences, and online communities. My work emphasizes mentorship, equity in STEM, and creating pathways for students who may not otherwise see themselves represented in science.

Outreach & Mentorship

  • Outreach Coordinator, Let’s Talk Science (2025– )

    Lead the University of Toronto Mississauga site, organizing STEM programs for schools and community organizations across the GTA. Recruit, train, and mentor over 100 student volunteers annually. Manage logistics and partnerships for flagship events such as Science Rendezvous and the Let’s Talk Science Challenge, collectively reaching more than 200 youth each year. Responsible for budgeting, program design, and ensuring equitable access for underserved schools.

  • Sci High Graduate Communicator, GTA (2025)

    Delivered interactive classroom talks on molecular biology and genetics to high school students. Focused on making research concepts approachable, inspiring students to engage with university-level science, and encouraging equity and representation in STEM pathways.

  • STEM Mentor, Letters to a Pre-Scientist (2024– )

    Provide long-term mentorship to middle school students in underserved communities through pen-pal exchanges. Share stories of daily life as a researcher, offer advice on navigating educational pathways, and encourage the pursuit of curiosity-driven science. Help students connect STEM to their own interests and goals, building confidence and representation in the sciences.

  • Communications Assistant, Women’s Health Research Cluster (2024–2025)

    Created accessible science communication materials to highlight equity issues in women’s health and reproductive justice. Produced infographics and social media content, translating research for both academic and general audiences. Supported knowledge mobilization initiatives that promote diverse perspectives in health research and advocate for systemic change.
