Projects

This page highlights selected academic research and communication projects. For code and data pipelines, see my GitHub ✿

Doctoral Research

  • Epigenetic Regulation of Memory — University of Toronto (2025– )

    PhD project exploring how histone variant exchange and chromatin remodeling shape memory-associated gene expression. Uses rodent behavioural models, molecular assays, and genomic tools to investigate mechanisms relevant to Alzheimer’s disease.

  • Lysosomal Biology & Neurodegeneration

    Investigating how lysosome abundance and function interact with synaptic plasticity and cognition. Integrates molecular neuroscience and cellular clearance pathways.

Undergraduate & Fellowship Research

  • Postpartum Depression & Neuroinflammation — CAMH (2025)

    Studied behavioural and neural changes in rodent models of postpartum depression. Combined pharmacological interventions (SSRIs, cytokine blockade) with behavioural assays. Results presented as a poster at CAMH conference.

  • Metacommunity Biodiversity Models — McMaster University (2024–2025)

    Built agent-based models in NetLogo and Python to test how patch size and connectivity influence biodiversity. Presented findings at Ontario Biology Day, McMaster Symposium, and WISE Conference.

    Code on GitHub
  • Arabidopsis Immunity — McMaster University (2023–2024)

    Supported research on systemic acquired resistance and age-related resistance. Gained lab skills in RNA extraction, PCR, and pathogen assays.

  • Amphibian Conservation — Operation Wallacea, Honduras (2023)

    Conducted amphibian biodiversity surveys and chytrid fungal surveillance in cloud forest ecosystems. Authored a report on fungal emergence threats to amphibian fauna.

Science Communication Projects

  • Women’s Health Research Cluster (2024–2025)

    Created infographics, blog posts, and social media campaigns on equity issues in women’s health and reproductive justice. Supported knowledge mobilization to broader audiences.

  • Let’s Talk Science Outreach (2025– )

    Organized and led large-scale STEM outreach events, including Science Rendezvous and Let’s Talk Science Challenge. Designed hands-on STEM demos for K–12 classrooms and communities.
